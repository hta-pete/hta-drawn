Meet Hightower.

We're as old as the world wide web.

We opened our doors in 1990 as the nation’s first full-service advertising agency specializing in truck driver recruiting. That’s the same year the prototype for the Internet was first developed. Coincidence? We think not.

Besides being a technologically savvy company, we’re also a family company, established by the late Elizabeth Hightower and still run today by her brothers Eddie, Pat and Jack. Headquartered in Madison, Mississippi, we have offices in Garrison, Iowa and St. Louis, Missouri. Financially stable and debt free, we’ve twice been recognized as a “Fast 40” company by the Mississippi Business Journal and received a Gold Davey Award for graphic design excellence.

As the agency of record for a host of the nation’s leading carriers, our professional credentials include membership in the Truckload Carriers Association, The National Association of Publicly Funded Truck Driving Schools, and the Truck Writers National Association.

Our Mission

We seek to provide our clients with the highest quality, data-driven advertising and ad placement in the industry.
